# 03. CARDIOVASCULAR

> VO2 max is one of the top 3 predictors of all-cause mortality. Elite-level VO2 max cuts mortality risk by ~5x vs. low fitness.

---

| Percentile | Male 20–29 | Male 30–39 | Male 40–49 | Female 20–29 | Female 30–39 | Female 40–49 |

| 50th | 48 | 44 | 41 | 37 | 34 | 31 |

**Your target:** Top 25% for your age. Above 50 is associated with sharply lower mortality risk.
