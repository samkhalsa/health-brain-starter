## Neuroprotective Habits Checklist

Daily or weekly habits associated with slower cognitive decline.

- [ ] 7.5+ hrs sleep (memory consolidation happens in sleep)
- [ ] Zone 2 cardio 150+ min/week (BDNF, cerebral blood flow)
- [ ] Strength training 2+ x/week (muscle mass predicts brain health)
- [ ] Novel cognitive challenge (new language, instrument, skill)
- [ ] Social connection — 3+ meaningful interactions/week
- [ ] Omega-3 intake (fatty fish 2x/week or supplement)
- [ ] Mediterranean / MIND diet pattern
- [ ] Meditation / mindfulness practice
- [ ] Sunlight / time outdoors
- [ ] No smoking, moderate or zero alcohol

---

## Sleep-Brain Connection

Deep sleep clears amyloid via the glymphatic system. Cross-reference your deep-sleep data in [09_recovery_hrv.md](09_recovery_hrv.md).

**Target:** 15–23% deep sleep (~1.5 hrs/night for an 8-hour sleep).

---

## Cognitive Red Flags (see a doctor)

- Persistent memory lapses that disrupt daily life
- Repeated difficulty finding words for common objects
- Getting lost in familiar places
- Significant personality changes
- Trouble with complex tasks (finances, planning) that were previously easy
