## Progressive Overload

Add 2.5–5 lb to compound lifts every 1–2 weeks. When you can't, add a rep. When you can't add a rep, add a set. When you can't add a set, deload 10% for a week and restart.

---

## Recovery-Adjusted Training

Based on wearable recovery score:

| Recovery | Training response |
|----------|------------------|
| **Green (>66%)** | Full intensity, push PRs |
| **Yellow (33–66%)** | Target weights but reduce volume 20% |
| **Red (<33%)** | Active recovery only — walk, mobility, light cardio |
