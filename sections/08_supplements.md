## Timing Rules

- **Morning:** Fat-soluble vitamins (D, K, A, E) with first fatty meal
- **Pre-workout:** Creatine (any time actually, but habit stacking), caffeine if using
- **With meals:** Omega-3, multivitamin, minerals that irritate empty stomach
- **Evening:** Magnesium (glycinate for sleep), any melatonin or l-theanine

---

## Quality Checklist

- [ ] Third-party tested (NSF, USP, Informed Sport, or IFOS for fish oil)
- [ ] No proprietary blends (hides dosing)
- [ ] Active form preferred (methylfolate over folic acid, MK-7 K2 over MK-4)
- [ ] Reasonable bottle size vs. expiration
- [ ] No fillers that trigger your allergies

---

## Review Cadence

- **Every 3 months:** retest any marker a supplement targets
- **Every 6 months:** reassess the whole stack — drop anything not earning its place
- **Any new addition:** start alone for 4 weeks so you can attribute effects
