# 05. MOBILITY

> **Joint-by-joint framework** (Gray Cook / Mike Boyle): each joint has a primary need — mobility or stability. Problems in one joint force compensation in the joints above and below.

## PAILs / RAILs Protocol

For any joint where ROM is limited and you're trying to expand it. Protocol per joint:

1. **Passive stretch:** 2 min at end range
2. **PAILs (Progressive Angular Isometric Loading):** 10s ramp to 70% effort, push into stretch, hold 10s, ramp down 10s
3. **RAILs (Regressive):** 10s ramp to 70% effort pulling *out* of the stretch, hold 10s, ramp down 10s
4. **Passive re-stretch:** 2 min at (ideally) expanded end range

2–4x/week per joint. Expect 2–4 weeks to see measurable ROM change.
