## Sleep Protocol

### Wind-down routine (60–90 min before bed)

- [ ] **3 hrs before bed:** last food
- [ ] **2 hrs before bed:** last caffeine well before (cutoff: 2pm)
- [ ] **1 hr before bed:** screens off or blue light blockers on
- [ ] **90–60 min before bed:** hot shower or bath (paradoxical cooling helps onset)
- [ ] **30 min before bed:** magnesium glycinate, reading, journal
- [ ] Room cool (65–68°F / 18–20°C)
- [ ] Room dark (blackout curtains, eye mask)
- [ ] Room quiet (white noise if needed)

### Morning anchor

- [ ] Wake at consistent time (±30 min, even weekends)
- [ ] Sunlight within 10 min of waking (outdoor > through-window > SAD lamp)
- [ ] Move body (walk, stretch) within 30 min

### Non-negotiables

- No alcohol within 3 hrs of bed (destroys REM)
- No heavy meals within 3 hrs of bed
- No phone in bedroom if you can manage it
