## The Plate Method

For every meal, visualize your plate:

- **½ plate:** non-starchy vegetables
- **¼ plate:** lean protein (palm-sized for women, two palms for men)
- **¼ plate:** smart carbs (sweet potato, quinoa, rice, oats) OR fruit
- **Thumb:** healthy fats (avocado, olive oil, nuts, seeds)
