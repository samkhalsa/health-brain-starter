## Root-Cause Chain

Injuries are rarely isolated. Draw the biomechanical chain for your specific issues.

Example:
```
Desk posture 8+ hrs/day
    │
    ├──► Tight hip flexors ──► Glute inhibition ──► Lumbar compensation
    │                                                     │
    │                                                     └──► Low back tightness
    ├──► Forward head posture ──► Deep neck flexor weakness
    │                                  │
    │                                  └──► Upper trap overuse
    └──► Rounded shoulders ──► Pec tightness ──► External rotator weakness
```

```

    │
    └──► [compensation] ──► [downstream issue]
```
