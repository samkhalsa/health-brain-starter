## PHASE 2: ADD COMPLEXITY (Weeks 5–8)

With the foundation locked, layer in more demanding work.

- Increase training frequency/intensity per [04_strength.md](04_strength.md)
- Dial in macros, not just protein — carbs, fat, fiber per [07_nutrition.md](07_nutrition.md)
- Add targeted supplements per [08_supplements.md](08_supplements.md)
- Begin mobility protocol per [05_mobility.md](05_mobility.md)
- Add recovery modalities (sauna, cold exposure, bodywork) per [09_recovery_hrv.md](09_recovery_hrv.md)

Retest everything at the end of Phase 3:

- [ ] Full bloodwork panel — compare to baseline in [01_bloodwork.md](01_bloodwork.md)

- [ ] Update dashboard in [HEALTH_BRAIN.md](../HEALTH_BRAIN.md)

Log every protocol change with outcome in [16_protocol_changes.md](16_protocol_changes.md).
