## ROOT CAUSE DIAGRAM

Draw the causal chain. Most health issues in young adults trace to a single keystone (often body fat, chronic stress, sleep debt, or specific nutrient deficiency).

```
[Keystone issue]
      │
      ├──► [Downstream marker 1] ──► [Symptom 1]
      ├──► [Downstream marker 2] ──► [Symptom 2]
      └──► [Downstream marker 3] ──► [Symptom 3]
```

Example — body fat as a keystone:
```
Excess body fat
      │
      ├──► Insulin resistance ──► Elevated HbA1c, fasting insulin
      ├──► Aromatization ──► Elevated estradiol, low free T
      ├──► Chronic inflammation ──► Elevated hs-CRP
      └──► Liver stress ──► Elevated ALT/AST
```
