## Red-Flag Quick Card

What should trigger an urgent doctor visit.

| Finding | Threshold | Action |
|---------|-----------|--------|
| Chest pain on exertion | Any | ER / urgent care |
| Unexplained weight loss | >10 lb in <3 months | MD within 2 weeks |
| HbA1c | >6.5% | MD within 1 month — likely diabetic |
| Blood pressure | >140/90 repeatedly | MD within 2 weeks |
| Resting HR | >100 or <40 (not athlete) | MD within 2 weeks |
| hs-CRP | >10 mg/L | Investigate infection / MD visit |
| ALT | >2x upper limit | MD — possible liver issue |
| Ferritin + Transferrin sat | Both elevated | Rule out hemochromatosis |
| Cortisol AM | Below range | Endo workup |
| Persistent fatigue | >4 weeks despite sleep | Full workup |
